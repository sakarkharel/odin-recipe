A Simple Guide to making Caprese Salad



Description

Caprese salad is a classic Italian dish renowned for its simplicity and fresh flavors. It features a combination of ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, fragrant basil leaves,
and a drizzle of high-quality extra virgin olive oil. The salad is typically assembled by layering slices of tomatoes and mozzarella cheese alternately on a plate, with basil leaves interspersed
between them. The vibrant colors of red, white, and green pay homage to the Italian flag, making this salad not only delicious but visually appealing as well. The creamy texture of the mozzarella
complements the juicy sweetness of the tomatoes, while the basil adds a refreshing herbal note. A generous drizzle of extra virgin olive oil enhances the flavors and adds richness to the dish.
Some variations may include a splash of balsamic vinegar for a tangy contrast or a sprinkle of salt and pepper for added seasoning. Caprese salad is a perfect representation of Italian cuisine's
emphasis on using fresh, high-quality ingredients to create dishes that are both simple and satisfying. It's a delightful appetizer or side dish that captures the essence of summer with every bite.

Ingredients

  • Fresh ripe tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into uniform slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving plate, alternating them.
  3. Tuck fresh basil leaves in between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  4. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the arranged salad.
  5. Optionally, you can add a drizzle of balsamic vinegar for extra flavor.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your simple and delicious Caprese Salad!
Enjoy!!!
