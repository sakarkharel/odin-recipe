MoMo, a delicious dumpling hailing from Nepal.



Description

Momo, a beloved dish originating from Nepal and popular across South Asia, is a delectable dumpling delicacy that tantalizes taste buds with its savory filling and delicate wrapper.
To prepare this culinary delight, begin by crafting a flavorful mixture of finely minced meat, often chicken, lamb, or pork, combined with aromatic spices such as garlic, ginger, and coriander.
This seasoned filling is then carefully encased in thin rounds of dough, made from flour and water, which are folded into intricate shapes resembling tiny purses. These little parcels are then
steamed to perfection, resulting in tender, juicy momos bursting with rich flavors. Typically served with a dipping sauce made from tomatoes, chili, and a dash of tangy lemon, momos
offer a delightful fusion of textures and tastes, making them a cherished favorite among food enthusiasts worldwide. Whether enjoyed as a comforting snack or a satisfying meal,
momos never fail to deliver a mouthwatering culinary experience that leaves diners craving for more.


Ingredients

  • For the filling:
    • Finely minced meat (chicken, lamb, pork, or a combination)
    • Finely chopped onions
    • Minced garlic
    • Minced ginger
    • Chopped green onions
    • Chopped cilantro
    • Salt to taste
    • Ground black pepper
    • Ground cumin
    • Ground coriander
    • Soy sauce (optional)

  • For the dough:
    • All-Purpose flour
    • Water
    • Salt

  • For the dipping sauce:
    • Tomatoes
    • Green chilies
    • Garlic cloves
    • Ginger
    • Lemon Juice
    • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling:
    1. Finely mince the meat of your choice (chicken, lamb, pork).
    2. Chop onions, garlic, ginger, green onions, and cilantro finely.
    3. In a mixing bowl, combine the minced meat with the chopped vegetables.
    4. Season the mixture with salt, ground black pepper, ground cumin, ground coriander, and soy sauce (if using). Mix well until evenly combined.

  2. Make the dough:
    1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour and a pinch of salt.
    2. Gradually add water and knead until a smooth, elastic dough forms.
    3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes.

  3. Assemble the momos:
    1. Divide the dough into small, equal-sized portions.
    2. Roll each portion into a thin circle on a lightly floured surface.
    3. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough circle.

  4. Shape the momos:
    1. To make pleats, fold one edge of the dough circle over the filling, then pinch and fold small sections of the dough along the edge to seal the momo.
    2. Repeat this process until you have pleated and sealed all the momos.

  5. Steam the momos:
    1. Arrange the shaped momos on a greased steaming tray or lined steamer basket, leaving some space between each momo.
    2. Steam the momos over boiling water for about 10-15 minutes or until the dough is cooked through and the filling is cooked.

  6. Make the diping sauce:
    1. Blend tomatoes, green chilies, garlic cloves, and ginger in a blender until smooth.
    2. Transfer the mixture to a saucepan and cook over medium heat.
    3. Add lemon juice and salt to taste, then simmer until the sauce thickens slightly.

  7. Serve:
    1. Once the momos are cooked, serve them hot with the prepared dipping sauce on the side.

Enjoy your delicious homemade momos!
